How it works

Voice first. Forms second.

1

Record

Resident taps record. Preceptor speaks naturally for 1-2 minutes about the encounter, strengths, and areas for growth.

2

Process

AI transcribes, de-identifies PHI, and extracts structured assessment mapped to CanMEDS competencies.

3

Review

Resident reviews auto-populated field notes with confidence scores. Edit, approve, and export as PDF.

Features

Built for medical education

Multi-form support

Auto-populate T-Res Field Notes, One45 Daily Evaluations, and more. One recording fills out whichever form your program uses.

T-Res + One45

PHI protection

Dual-pass scrubbing: fast regex catches names and MRNs, then a contextual LLM pass catches everything else. Zero PHI in storage.

Regex + LLM dual-pass

CanMEDS mapping

Feedback is automatically tagged to CanMEDS competency roles. See which skills your preceptor actually observed.
